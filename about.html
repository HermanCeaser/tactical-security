Company Intro

Tactical Security Services Limited is a well-established Security Company that offers secure solutions products to its client. Our History

Background

The company was set up after the realization that there was a need for a company that can adequately recognize and deal with any security challenges that may arise and apply the necessary skills to ensure the protection and security of lives and assets thus enabling clients to focus on their core business activities.

Geographic Spread

Tactical Security Services currently provides critical installation security services to its main clients in a number of districts spread all over Uganda. We provide security in Jinja, Tororo, Mbale, Soroti, Lira, Gulu, Arua, Fort Portal, Mubende, Mityana and Kasese, Yumbe, Isingiro. As such our geographic reach is wide enough to provide most clients with adequate security of their assets, lives and property wherever they may be.

Core business

We have as a consequence, developed our capacity in the provision and maintenance of security of critical installations and assets. Our understanding, experience and awareness of the Ugandan security and risk challenges gives us an unrivalled advantage over other competitors in the provision of a holistic security solution and enables the company to provide its clients with valuable up to date analysis and recommendations to enable them secure their facilities, critical assets and personal security
