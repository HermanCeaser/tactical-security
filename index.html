Our Services

We Offer A Variety Services

Perimeter Intrusion Detection System

We provide devices or sensors that detect the presence of an intruder attempting to breach the physical perimeter of a property, building, or other secured area.

Events Protection

We offer security detail at all your events. Corporate and Non-corporate events can all be catered for by our security team.

Access Control

This is a fundamental component of data security that dictates who's allowed to access and use company information and resources.

Self Defense Training

This is a set of awareness, assertiveness, verbal confrontation skills, safety strategies, and physical techniques that enable someone to successfully prevent, escape, resist and survive violent assaults.

Body Guard

We provide personal protection for a client, keeping them safe from physical threats like assault, theft, kidnapping, or assassination.

Counter Surveillance

This is the process of detecting and mitigating hostile surveillance, it is an important aspect of counterterrorism and security operations.
